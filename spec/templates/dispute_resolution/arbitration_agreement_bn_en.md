# Arbitration Agreement (English)

## 1. Agreement to Arbitrate
The Parties agree that any dispute arising out of or in connection with {{Underlying Agreement}} shall be finally resolved by arbitration.

## 2. Rules and Institution
Arbitration shall be conducted under {{Arbitration Rules/Institution}}.

## 3. Seat and Venue
The seat of arbitration shall be {{Seat}} in Bangladesh. Hearings may be held at {{Venue}} or by virtual means.

## 4. Number and Appointment of Arbitrators
The tribunal shall consist of {{Number of Arbitrators}} arbitrator(s). Appointment procedure: {{Appointment Procedure}}.

## 5. Language
The language of arbitration shall be {{Arbitration Language}}.

## 6. Interim Measures
The tribunal may grant interim relief. Parties may also seek interim measures from courts as permitted by the Arbitration Act, 2001.

## 7. Costs
Costs shall be allocated as determined by the tribunal.

## 8. Governing Law
The arbitration agreement is governed by the laws of Bangladesh.
